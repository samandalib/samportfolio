Time Travel

Time Travel | Virtual Reality

Background

Virtual reality or VR as a technology provides multiple affordances over any other accessible medium in the history of human civilization. Among the numerous applications that VR can be used for, education stands out as being one of the most natural and effective. In this project I stepped in learning basics of Virtual Reality and making a virtual experience for history education purposes


The main concept in this project originates in my interest in experiencing historical monuments and trying to imagine that context at the original time.

MY ROLES
 Ideation, Unity Modeling, C# scripts, Oculus deployment

MY TAKEAWAYS
 Understanding fundamentals of VR technology and learning how to create a project in Unity game engine and managing user interactions during the experience

FUTURE CHALLENGE
 The concept of time travel experience is more than physical representation of the space, working on human characters and creating a narrative during experience can help users have a better experience.

Project Process

In this project, the specific goal of our research is to find solutions and ideas to help students at the University of Texas at Austin to find a room in a building easily. The original prompt was broad and we had to identify our own focus area. Through the whole process of the project, we actively engaged the target user population by conducting contextual inquiries, secondary research, prototyping and multiple rounds of usability testing to understand their pain points and needs to inform the design decisions.








The project started with learning some fundamentals of VR technology and understanding how different pieces of technology and Human perception make a person feel present at a virtual world. We did some research on use cases of VR in education and other fields. We decided to work on the application of VR for historical preservation and education purposes. At the next step we started exploring some ideas to start working on our project. We came up with some use cases and we decided to work on a imaginary restaurant with historical background. The idea was to show the user the present time of a restaurant as well as the same restaurant in 50 years ago.


For this project, I learned and used Unity Game Engine software running on a computer and an Oculus Rift HMD. Using Unity, I created a virtual environment of a restaurant. For the experience, the user would be wearing an HMD, and using hand-held tracking devices. The tracking would mostly be of head motion and orientation (inertial), and optical for the hand-held devices. Also through the headphones in the Oculus headset, the player can hear ambient noises of multiple people talking in a crowded restaurant.



Measuring the Experience

We aksed about 12 users to test our virtual experience and had some feedback from each user on their feeling and their perception for the purpose of our project.
