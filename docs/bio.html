Hesam was born and raised in Isfahan, one of the ancient Middle-Eastern cities. The whole city is a musume of art and interesting stories from the past. Living in such great city is a great opportunity for learning about art, science and order. He started studying Architecture at the Art University of Isfahan, located right next to the famous Naqsh-e-Jahan square, which was a lifetime opportunity. He continued his studies in Project Management at National University of Iran at Tehran. He started his professional life as a Project Engineer at Green Volume Group and after 5 years established his personal design-build firm at Isfahan. He has designed and built several residential projects. His panchant for new technologies lead him towards understanding the basics of computer science and programming and aims at making a connection between his assets about art and architecture and the computer technology.
