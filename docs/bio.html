Hesam was born and raised in Isfahan, one of the ancient cities cities in the middle of Iran. The whole city is a musume of art and interesting stories from the past. Living in such great city is a great opportunity for learning about the nature of art, science and order. He started studying Architecture at the Art University of Isfahan, located right next to the famous Naqsh-e-Jahan square. He continued his studies in Project Management at National University of Iran at Tehran. He has designed and built several residential projects. His panchant for new technologies lead him towards understanding the basics of computer science and programming and aims at making a connection between his assets about art and architecture and the computer technology.
