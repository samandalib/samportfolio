Site Properties
Site of the project is the lands above the Resalat Tunnel that is located at the center of Tehran. Resalat Tunnel is one of the main east-west streams of Tehran, however it has mostly affected subterranean layers of urban space and its impact hasn’t extended to the surface of the ground over the tunnel. The land over the Resalat Tunnel, considering their special location, could play an important role in creating an east-west connection crossing some of the important north-south channels of the city.

Plaza
One of the most prominent strengths of the site is the open area, lining Valiasr Street. Creating ‘public gathering spaces’ is known as one of the most important design elements in the principals stated at ‘the charter of new urbanism (CNU). In this charter, establishing these spaces in important sites is identified as a step towards making a social identity and encouragement of a culture of freedom.

.

Boundry
In order to reduce the impact of discordancy of the surrounded built environment, a buffer zone structure is defined as a light divider. This semi-transparent addition reduces the unpleasant visual effect of built environment on the site and offers an opportunity for creating a space with more sense of calmness and safety. This buffer zone can also be used for specific cultural and commercial purposes.
