IRCEO Lorestan
This project is an architectural proposal in response to the national call for architectural design for the Contstruction Engineers Society Building at Lorestan province. The design site is located in the Khoramabad city, where it is mostly recognized for the mountainous geography and cultural aspect of Lor race. This building proposal aims at representing the concept of engineering and design aligned with the cultural and geographical properties of its context.
