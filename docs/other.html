Professional Meetings

First Meeting: Architectural SMEs Management
November 2015

Special Guests: Dr. Ramin Madani, Ali Sheikh-al-eslam, Shervin Hoseini, Ehsan Hoseini
In this meeting professionals discussed different approaches that can be applied by architects to manage their firms. At the second part there was a QA between audiences and these professionals.

Second Meeting: Client's Management
December 2015

Special Guests: Mohammad Arab, Amir Mohammad Akhavan, Iman Aminlari
In this meeting professionals discussed different approaches that can be applied by architects to manage their clients in order to successfully reach their goals in their projects. At the second part there was a QA between audiences and these professionals and other approaches discussed as well.

Third Meeting: Lessons Learned from ICC project
February 2016

Special Guest: Mohammad Reza Ghazizadeh
In this meeting the experiences of Mr. Ghazizadeh, the chief architect of Isfahan City Center project was discussed. Working at a mega project is an experience that might be so unkown for many architects. In this meeting professional architects listened to the headings of Mr. Ghazizadeh's experiences and asked their questions as well.

Forth Meeting: Lessons Learned from A project in China
July 2016

Special Guest: Mohammad Shamaizadeh
In this meeting the experiences of Mr. Shamaizadeh for disigning and constructing a project in China was discussed. The project named 'IRAN's Garden' is a project for architectural expo. Mr. Shamaizadeh explained various challenges they faced in the process of having the project built. At the second part, participants asked their questions.

Fifth Meeting: Professional Manifesto
July 2016

Special Guest: Dr. Mohammad Reza Ghanei
In this meeting the subject of professional Manifesto was discussed. The purpose of this meeting was to see how practitioners in architecture field arrange their activities around their vision to the practice. at the first part, an exclusive recorded interview with four famous architectural firms was presented. At the second pard, Dr. Ghanei, the CEO of Polsheer Consultants presented the professional manifesto of their firm and discussed with the audience.

Sixth Meeting: City Scan
April 2017


This meeting aimed at exploring what is happening among professional architects in the city of Isfahan, with a focus on ideas about building facades. At the first part of the meeting two architects presented some views on the subject of facade design and regulations. On the second part more than ten architects shortly explained their approaches towars designing facades.
