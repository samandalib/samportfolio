Sama
Sama is a ceremony mostly recognized as whirling dervishes.They are the followers of Rumi, a Persian poet who lived in 13th century. Rumi believed passionately in the use of music, poetry and dance as a path for reaching God. It was from his ideas that the practice of whirling Dervishes developed into a ritual form.

Orientation
The Sama ceremony represents a mystical journey of man's spiritual ascent through mind and love to "Perfect".

Geometry
Sama ceremony defines a circular geometry for the place where it is held.

Light
The light should create a place for meditation and provide a place with a sense of deep thinking.

Bending the Light

The light moves towards a straight path. However, it does not mean that we alwasy obey the physics and do not think of bending the rules. In this design by bending a thin transparent plastic sheet and turning it to the form of a funnel will change everything. The twirling light is born. A light effect that can appropriately represent the concepts of Concentration, meditation, and deep thinking.
