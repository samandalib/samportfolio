CV | Works

I am currently a graduate student at the Information School at UT Austin. I started my professional life as an architecture practitioner and project engineer in the construction industry. I co-founded a design-build startup that was active until early 2019 when I decided to continue my education in Information School. I enjoy spending my time and energy to work on solving problems that require research, design, scientific approach, usability test, and adjustment. Seeing the result of the design and learning from the user's experiences in using the design product is what I'm looking for. In my method of work, the quality of the design process to the result is as important as the final product and it requires the existence of a management method that values the process as much as the result. I wish you a great experience while exploring my works and I will deeply appreciate it if you provide me with your valuable feedback by sending an email to handalib@utexas.edu.
